What Is Kickball?

Learned by many North Americans in primary school, kickball is played on a baseball diamond with a rubber ball. In place of a baseball bat, players used their feet to kick the ball from home plate, and attempt to run counterclockwise around the bases, while the opposing team tries to get them out by tagging them or the base ahead of the play.
If this still makes no sense, no worries- We run a clinic ahead of the season to try to familiarize you. Plus there are tons of people who will help you learn.

About Us

The Amsterdam Social Kickball League (ASKL) is a co-ed, adults only social league & is the first of its kind in the Netherlands. ASKL is all about meeting new people & having fun.
